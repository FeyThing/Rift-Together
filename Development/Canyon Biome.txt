The canyon biome is a new variation of desert. Which makes it capable of spawning and attracting dustdevils. This biome is mostly made up of randomly discarded pieces of scrap littered around due to the constant special meteor shower dumpings, and cactus. 

Some pools of water can be found in these canyons but overall it is desolate, besides the infestation of swarmers.
